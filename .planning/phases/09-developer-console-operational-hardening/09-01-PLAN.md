Build all new and extended control-plane API endpoints that the console pages will consume: analytics aggregation (usage, spend, errors grouped by model/key/endpoint/time), invoice list and detail, budget threshold CRUD with email notification dispatch, cursor-based ledger pagination, and a public model catalog endpoint.
