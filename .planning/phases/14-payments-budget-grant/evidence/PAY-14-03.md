# PAY-14-03 — Spend alerts at 50/80/100 thresholds with email + webhook delivery

## Truth

Workspace owners configure threshold alerts (50/80/100 % of soft cap) with
either email or HMAC-signed webhook delivery. A scheduled evaluator
detects threshold crossings against month-to-date BDT spend and dispatches
exactly one notification per crossing per period.

## Implementation

- Schema: `public.spend_alerts (id, workspace_id, threshold_pct CHECK in
  (50,80,100), delivery_channel CHECK in ('email','webhook'), webhook_url,
  last_fired_period DATE)`.
- `budgets.CronEvaluator.EvaluateBudgets(ctx, now)` walks workspaces with
  budgets, computes MTD spend (math/big), evaluates threshold crossings
  via `budgets.ThresholdCrossed`, and invokes the notifier.
- `budgets.Notifier` ports email (SMTP placeholder) + webhook (HMAC SHA-256
  signature header `X-Hive-Signature`). Webhook secret per workspace.
- `spendalerts.Runner` is the scheduler loop — interval-driven, eager-first
  pass on Start, idempotent Stop, race-free goroutine teardown (FIX-14-CI
  patch on 2026-05-07).

## Tests

- `TestEvaluator_FiresExactlyOnceAtThreshold` — 50/80/100 each.
- `TestEvaluator_MultipleThresholdsFireIndependently` — 50,80,100 all
  cross at 100 % MTD.
- `TestEvaluator_SoftCapZeroDisablesAlerts` — soft=0 short-circuits.
- `TestNotifier_WebhookSendsHMAC` — signature header verified on the
  receiving stub.
- `TestRunner_StartStop_TicksAtInterval` — eager + interval-driven passes.
