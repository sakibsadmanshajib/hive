## Objective
Close the public embeddings identity gap by making `text-embedding-3-small` the canonical catalog id, keeping `text-embedding-ada-002` as a compatibility alias, and ensuring provider-namespaced ids stay in `x-provider-model` rather than leaking into public response bodies.

## Context
- `12-CONTEXT.md` locks the public embeddings id to `text-embedding-3-small`, keeps `text-embedding-ada-002` as a compatibility alias, and forbids leaking `openai/text-embedding-3-small` through `CreateEmbeddingResponse.model`.
- `12-RESEARCH.md` confirmed the current gap: `ModelService` still catalogs `openai/text-embedding-3-small`, `resolveModelAlias()` still points `text-embedding-ada-002` at that provider id, and `ProviderRegistry.embeddings()` currently returns `body.model = result.providerModel`.
- `.planning/v1.0-MILESTONE-AUDIT.md` identifies the exact broken flow: the real runtime rejects or mis-identifies `client.embeddings.create({ model: "text-embedding-3-small" })` even though the mock regression harness passed.
- Keep this phase embeddings-specific. Do not add `text-embedding-3-large`, do not redesign the full model catalog, and do not move alias logic into `routes/embeddings.ts`.
