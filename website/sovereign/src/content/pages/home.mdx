## Proof spine

The architecture is the proof. Open model weights run entirely on your hardware,
inference never calls an external API, and no prompt, response, or document ever
leaves your network boundary.

<Claim id="perplexity-self-host">
  Perplexity runs DeepSeek R1 on its own servers in US and EU data centres. User
  data never leaves Western servers.
</Claim>

## Economics

The same open weights that keep your data in your building also cut the bill.
Open-weight output runs roughly ten to thirty times cheaper per million tokens
than proprietary frontier APIs.

<Claim id="pinterest-qwen">
  Pinterest cut AI costs roughly 90 percent and raised accuracy roughly 30
  percent by running and customising the open-weight Qwen3-VL model on its own
  infrastructure.
</Claim>

Results reflect Pinterest's specific implementation. Your outcomes will depend
on your hardware, model choice, and workload.
